How to Use the Forgetting Engine Demo 
Access: Click "Proof" in the header or visit /demo 
Quick Start: 
1. Choose a preset (Simple, TSP Routes, or Protein Folding) 
2. Click "Run Algorithm" to see the FE work 
3. Watch the results: 
• 
Survivors = made it through 
• 
Paradoxes = retained despite low fitness (highlighted in gold) 
• 
Eliminated = discarded 
Try This:

Toggle "Enable Paradox Retention" OFF and run again 
• 
Notice paradoxes disappear 
• 
Toggle it back ON to see the difference
